About us

Pet health is at the heart of everything we do. Every pet we work with gets the same high quality care.

Our Story

We have been providing veterinary services for pets in and around rural Manchester for the last 20 years. Specialising in dog care we also provide services for a range of pets including cats, rabbits, horses and guinea pigs. We aim to provide our customers with a friendly and helpful service to ensure that our client’s pets remain happy and healthy. Our clinic has built a strong reputation for being one of Manchester’s best Vets.

About Noah

Noah, the Managing Director of Noah’s pet clinic, has wanted to work with animals his whole life. Since graduating from The University of Manchester he has been working with animals of all types, especially dogs. Noah wanted to create Noah’s Pet Clinic because he wanted to make animal care accessible to everyone in the local area.

About our staff

We have an excellent team of vet doctors and nurses who provide effective and attentive care for your pets. We only hire highly trained staff and people that fit in with our company ethos to ensure that every pet and pet owner gets the best service possible.
